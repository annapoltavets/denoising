## Notes

- The book’s central point is that **real photographic and video noise is usually not well described by plain AWGN**.
- In practice, sensor noise is often **signal-dependent**, sometimes **correlated**, may include **clipping/saturation**, and may also contain **structured components** such as fixed-pattern or line noise.
- For implementation, the book spans:
  - classical transform and statistical methods,
  - patch-based and non-local approaches,
  - video denoising,
  - industry camera-pipeline perspectives,
  - and CNN-based restoration methods.
